About Us

At Y&S, we believe that a fragrance should be as unique as the person wearing it. Specializing in compound perfumes, we craft personalized scents that reflect your personality, mood, and style. Using high-quality ingredients and artistic blending techniques, we create perfumes that leave a lasting impression — whether you prefer floral, woody, spicy, or fresh notes. Whether you're looking for your signature scent or a special gift, Y&S is your destination for handcrafted, custom-blended perfumes made with care and creativity.
